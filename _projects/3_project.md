With the growing reliance on online group chats for team collaboration, researchers are examining how CAs can support these interactions. CAs utilize natural language processing to analyze group dynamics and perform timely interventions, such as sending facilitation messages to encourage balanced participation. You can learn about my work in my [dissertation](https://www.ideals.illinois.edu/items/127715.)
